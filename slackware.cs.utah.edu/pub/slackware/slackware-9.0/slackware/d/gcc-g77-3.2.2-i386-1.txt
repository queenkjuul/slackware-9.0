gcc-g77: This package contains those parts of the compiler collection needed to
gcc-g77: compile Fortran code.  The base gcc package is also required.  GNU
gcc-g77: Fortran supports a variety of extensions to, and dialects of, the
gcc-g77: Fortran language.  Its primary base is the ANSI FORTRAN 77 standard,
gcc-g77: with some some extensions popularized by the 'f77' and 'f2c' compilers,
gcc-g77: other compilers (such as Digital products), and the Fortran 90
gcc-g77: standard.
