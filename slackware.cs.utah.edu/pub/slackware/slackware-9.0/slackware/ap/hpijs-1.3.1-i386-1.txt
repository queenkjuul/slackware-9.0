hpijs: hpijs (Hewlett-Packard inkjet drivers for Ghostscript)
hpijs:
hpijs: The Hewlett-Packard Inkjet Driver Project (HPIJS) is a add-on printer
hpijs: driver for GNU Ghostscript.  There are two parts to the IJS interface:
hpijs: the IJS client that resides in GNU Ghostscript, and the IJS server
hpijs: (in this package).  HPIJS runs as a server or co-process to GNU
hpijs: Ghostscript. The server is spawned automatically by GNU Ghostscript.
hpijs:
hpijs: There are currently more than 80 HP inkjet printer models supported
hpijs: with HPIJS.
