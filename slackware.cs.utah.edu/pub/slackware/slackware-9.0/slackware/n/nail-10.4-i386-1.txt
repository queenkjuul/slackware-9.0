nail: nail (a simple mail client)
nail:
nail: Nail is a mail user agent derived from Berkeley Mail 8.1 and is
nail: intended to provide the functionality of the POSIX.2 mailx command
nail: with additional support for MIME messages, SSL/TLS, POP3 and SMTP.
nail:
nail: The maintainer and primary developer of nail is Gunnar Ritter.
